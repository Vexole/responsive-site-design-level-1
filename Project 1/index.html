About Me

I am Bhupesh Shrestha, an international student from Nepal. I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Engineering from Kathmandu University in 2017; since then, I started working as a software developer until I decided to narrow down my career as a Web Developer and continue my education.



Since my college days, I have been involved in coding, and I am passionate about it. I liked building web applications and exploring new technologies and tools in the market. With that interest, I joined my Bachelor's degree to gain a better and more profound knowledge of coding and computers in general. I read books about ways to structure the project and implement clean code practices. When coding, I compared the different technologies to get the optimal product as an outcome. And as a professional, I have worked on many projects of distinct domains with people from all over the world and witnessed different cultures implemented when developing solutions for problems.



I once had the opportunity to experience leading a team and dealing with clients. I relished the experience of being a Team Lead and learned a lot from it to better my career. Since then, I decided to learn more ways to develop flexible and scalable products and help produce better outcomes. With exposure to different circumstances, I have enhanced the primary traits of a developer, such as the capability to solve complex problems, a better understanding of task planning and execution, tackle tense situations, deal with clients, provide appropriate solutions, and guide the juniors.
